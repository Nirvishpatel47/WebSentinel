About Vertex Creative

Our Story
From Freelancers to Full Agency

Vertex Creative was founded in 2012 by Sara Reinhart and Marcus Kim in a small studio in Portland, Oregon. What started as a two-person design shop has grown into a 48-person agency with clients ranging from funded startups to Fortune 500 companies. Our philosophy has never changed: honest work, thoughtful design, and real results.

Timeline
Our Journey

Founded in Portland

Sara and Marcus start the agency with 2 clients and a vision for honest, impactful design.
