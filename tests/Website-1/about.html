Our Story

15 Years of Transforming Businesses

Founded in London in 2009, Nexus Consulting was built on a single belief: that strategy without execution is just a document.

Over 15 years we've grown from a three-person boutique to a global consultancy with offices across London, New York, Singapore, and Dubai. Our 120+ consultants bring deep sector expertise across manufacturing, fintech, sustainability, healthcare, and consumer goods.

Every engagement begins with listening — understanding your context, your constraints, and your ambitions. We then design and deliver solutions that are pragmatic, measurable, and built to last.
